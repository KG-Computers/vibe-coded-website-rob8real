Personal Data Protection

At Eel Pool, we are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This policy outlines our strict rules regarding personal data:

1. Data Privacy

All personal data, including usernames, account information, and investment activity, is strictly confidential. Users must keep all personal information to themselves and respect the privacy of other users.

2. Prohibition on Data Sharing

You are NOT allowed to:

  • Share, publish, or distribute any other user's personal data or information
  • Screenshot or capture other users' personal information
  • Attempt to identify anonymous investors or users
  • Share login credentials or account access with others
  • Use personal data for any purpose outside of this platform

3. Unauthorized Access

Any attempt to gain unauthorized access to personal data or information will result in:

  • Immediate and permanent ban from the site
  • Removal of all your pitches and investments
  • Potential reporting to school administrators or authorities

4. What We Collect

We only collect the minimum information necessary:

  • Username (displayed publicly)
  • Password (encrypted and never shared)
  • Account type (student/teacher/operator)
  • Investment and pitch activity

5. Anonymous Features

All investments and comments are displayed anonymously to protect user privacy. Do not attempt to identify or expose anonymous users.

6. Your Responsibilities

By using Eel Pool, you agree to:

  • Keep your password secure and private
  • Respect the privacy of all users
  • Report any privacy violations or security concerns
  • Use the platform responsibly and ethically
